Why It Works

People don't buy books from ads.
They buy from people.

You are not the best person to talk about your book. Not because you're not credible, because you're the author. People expect you to say it's great.

When a BookTok creator with 40,000 followers says "I couldn't put this down," that's a sale. Possibly fifty sales. That's what we're building toward.

The hook library and creator brief are what make this possible. Creators don't need to figure out what to say about your book. We hand them the angles and let them make it their own.

The Hook Library
10 angles pulled from your book: the surprising ideas, the things people get wrong, the lines that stop a scroll. This is what creators actually use.
The Creator Brief
A one-page document we send to influencers and reviewers. Who the book is for, what makes it different, suggested angles, one paragraph synopsis. Freedom within a framework.
The Outreach List
The specific BookTok creators, Bookstagram accounts, podcasts, and book clubs that reach your exact reader. We find them, contact them, and follow up.
The Only Rule
Creators need freedom. The tighter you control the message, the worse they perform. Honest reviews, even critical ones, build more trust than controlled praise.
